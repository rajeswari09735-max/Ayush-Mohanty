# Janapada & Mahajanapada Strategy Game

## Overview

This game is an interactive historical strategy experience based on the ancient **Janapadas and Mahajanapadas of India**. The player takes the role of a ruler and governs a kingdom by making important political and moral decisions.

The game is designed to make the study of early Indian history engaging by allowing players to experience leadership, justice, diplomacy, and conquest in a simulated historical setting.

## Gameplay

1. Start the application.
2. Click **Start**.
3. Choose the **Janapada or Mahajanapada** you want to rule.
4. Select the **dynasty**.
5. The player becomes the **first king of that dynasty**.

Example:

* If the player chooses **Magadha** and the **Maurya dynasty**, the player becomes **Chandragupta Maurya**.

## Game Decisions

During the game the ruler must make decisions related to governing the kingdom. These include options such as:

* War
* Peace
* Justice
* Administration / Governance

Each decision leads to different scenarios and challenges.

## Justice System

In justice mode, the king must solve cases presented by the people of the kingdom.
For example:

* A dispute between citizens
* A criminal case
* A problem affecting the kingdom

The player must decide the correct judgement to maintain fairness and stability.

## Rewards System

If the player governs wisely and makes good decisions, they are rewarded with **coins** and gain prestige as a successful ruler.

## Conquest Mode

The game also includes a **conquest mode** where the player expands the kingdom through strategic decisions and battles.

## Inspiration

The game is inspired by the political and historical developments of ancient India, especially the rise of powerful kingdoms such as **Magadha** and the **Mauryan Empire**.
